## 项目概述 / Project Overview

- **项目名称**：冬日灵感季 / SeaArt Winter Inspiration Season
- **标签**：Campaign · Holiday · Tri-lingual
- **配色**：`#6E1B1C`（深酒红）/ `#FFB94B`（暖金）
- **我的角色**：系列活动视觉统筹 / Visual lead for the full campaign series

### 项目简介（用于 project-header 区域）

**中文**：
2024 年末跨越圣诞至新年的系列创作活动，包含「冬日灵感季」主活动与「共创圣诞奇迹」子活动两个阶段，覆盖中、英、日三语市场。从主视觉定义到各阶段 Banner、活动详情页与社媒物料，完成全链路视觉交付。

**English**:
A multi-phase holiday creation campaign spanning Christmas to New Year 2024, comprising the main "Winter Inspiration Season" and the sub-event "Christmas Miracle Co-Creation." Delivered full visual pipeline — from key visual to phase-specific banners, detail pages, and social media assets — across Chinese, English, and Japanese markets.

## Section 1: 主视觉与视觉策略 / Key Visual & Design Strategy

> 对应展板顶部区域：Hero 主视觉 + 项目背景 + 标题字体 + 配色方案
> 建议裁图范围：展板从顶部到「Event Banner」标题之前

**中文**：
以一棵装饰华丽的圣诞树作为核心视觉锚点，配合礼盒、缎带等节日元素搭建画面主体。深酒红背景营造温暖而高级的节日氛围，与暖金色高亮形成对比，避免落入常见圣诞设计的「红绿配」俗套。标题采用手写体英文（SeaArt Winter Inspiration Season）与中文书法字（冬日灵感季）双版本，在不同语言场景中保持调性统一。

**English**:
A lavishly decorated Christmas tree serves as the visual anchor, surrounded by gift boxes and ribbon elements that build the scene. The deep wine-red background creates a warm yet premium holiday atmosphere, paired with warm-gold highlights to avoid the cliché red-green Christmas palette. The title is set in both calligraphic English ("SeaArt Winter Inspiration Season") and Chinese brush script ("冬日灵感季"), maintaining tonal consistency across language contexts.

---

## Section 2: 活动横幅 / Event Banners

> 对应展板「Event Banner / 活动横幅」区域：左右两张 Banner 并排
> 建议裁图范围：两张 Banner 区域
> 左 = 中文版（冬日灵感季，12.10 - 12.26）
> 右 = 英文版（SeaArt Winter Inspiration Season，08/12 - 31/12）

**中文**：
基于同一套主视觉延展出中文与英文两个版本的活动横幅。中文版信息层级更紧凑——「冬日灵感季」主标题与活动时间「12.10 - 12.26」前置；英文版则采用更舒展的竖排标题节奏，配合「#Christmas」话题标签强化社交传播属性。两版共享同一棵圣诞树主体和暖色光效，但在排版密度与阅读动线上做了语言差异化处理。

**English**:
Two banner variants — Chinese and English — extended from the same key visual. The Chinese version adopts a tighter information hierarchy with the event title "冬日灵感季" and dates "12.10 – 12.26" placed upfront. The English version uses a more spacious vertical title rhythm with a "#Christmas" hashtag to boost social shareability. Both share the same Christmas tree hero and warm lighting, while layout density and reading flow are independently tuned for each language.

## Section 3: 活动详情页 / Activity Details Page

> 对应展板「Activity Details Page / 活动详情页」区域：三屏手机竖版页面
> 建议裁图范围：三张手机屏截图
> 左 = 活动首屏（标题 + 时间 + 阶段说明）
> 中 = 规则与奖励（Step 1/2 + Rewards + Category）
> 右 = 英文版参与流程与二维码

**中文**：
活动详情页承载了完整的参与链路：首屏延续主视觉氛围，快速传达活动时间与「Phase Two: Christmas Creative Feast」阶段说明；中段将参与步骤拆解为 Step 1 → Step 2 → Rewards 的线性流程，搭配 Category 分类入口引导不同创作方向；尾部放置二维码与社群入口完成转化闭环。深色底 + 暖光卡片的对比确保长页面的阅读节奏不单调。

**English**:
The activity detail page carries the full participation funnel: the hero screen extends the key visual atmosphere, instantly communicating event dates and the "Phase Two: Christmas Creative Feast" phase breakdown. The middle section decomposes participation into a Step 1 → Step 2 → Rewards linear flow, with Category entry points guiding users toward different creative directions. The footer closes the loop with QR codes and community links. Dark backgrounds paired with warm-glow content cards maintain reading rhythm across the long-scroll page.

## Section 4: 系列活动物料 / Series of Activities

> 对应展板「Series of Activities / 系列活动」区域：上下两排共 8 张 Banner
> 建议裁图范围：系列活动区域整体，或拆为上排（横版）+ 下排（竖版）
> 内容 = 「共创圣诞奇迹 / SeaArt Christmas Miracle Special Event」子活动
> 语言 = 英文 / 中文 / 日文（~クリスマスの奇跡を作ろう~）

**中文**：
「共创圣诞奇迹」作为系列活动的第二阶段，需要在延续「冬日灵感季」视觉基因的同时建立独立辨识度。保留了圣诞树与暖光氛围，但将标题字体从书法体切换为更具手写活力的风格，配色也增加了更多金色占比以区分阶段。物料覆盖英文（SeaArt Christmas Miracle Special Event）、中文（共创圣诞奇迹 特别活动）、日文（~クリスマスの奇跡を作ろう~）三语，横版与竖版两种比例，适配 Banner 位与社媒场景。

**English**:
"Christmas Miracle Co-Creation," the campaign's second phase, needed its own identity while staying within the "Winter Inspiration Season" visual family. The Christmas tree and warm lighting carry over, but the title shifts from calligraphic to a more hand-drawn, energetic style, with an increased gold ratio to distinguish the phase. Materials span three languages — English ("SeaArt Christmas Miracle Special Event"), Chinese ("共创圣诞奇迹 特别活动"), and Japanese ("~クリスマスの奇跡を作ろう~") — in both landscape and portrait formats for banner placements and social media.

---

## 项目总结（用于 project-footer 区域）

**中文**：
这个项目的核心挑战是在「统一」与「区分」之间找到平衡——系列活动需要家族感，但每个阶段又必须有自己的辨识度。最终通过「共享视觉元素（圣诞树 + 暖光）+ 差异化标题风格与配色比例」的策略解决了这个问题。三语适配也不是简单的文字替换，而是针对中、英、日不同的排版传统做了独立的版式优化。

**English**:
The core challenge was balancing "unity" with "distinction" — the campaign series needed family resemblance, yet each phase required its own identity. This was resolved through a strategy of shared visual elements (Christmas tree + warm glow) combined with differentiated title styles and color ratios. Tri-lingual adaptation went beyond text replacement, with independent layout optimization tailored to Chinese, English, and Japanese typographic conventions.
